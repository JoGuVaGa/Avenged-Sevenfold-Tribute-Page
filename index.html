Avenged Sevenfold is an heavy metal band from Huntington, California that was formed in 1999. The line up now days consist of M. Shadows as the vocalist, Zacky Vengeance as rythm guitarist and backing vocalist, Synyster Gates as lead guitarist and backing vocalist, Johnny Christ as bassist and backing vocalist and Brooks Wackerman as drummer.
